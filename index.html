Who We Are

Reliability Meets Exceptional Craftsmanship

Founded by Mitch and Phil — two directors with a passion for precision and a deep commitment to their clients — Elite Builders London is a high-quality building company based in South East London. We specialise in both residential and commercial projects across Greater London, from simple refurbishments to large-scale new builds.

Our team includes project managers, architects, designers, and skilled tradespeople from every discipline — meaning you never need to juggle multiple contractors. One team. Total accountability. Outstanding results.
